Maharaja Agrasen Institute App

July 2025 – August 2025
What

A fully functional Android application for MAIT college — bridging communication between students, faculty, and administrators through a single, unified platform.

Why

The institute lacked a centralized digital touchpoint. Students and faculty had no streamlined way to access notices, events, or department info — this app solved that gap.

How

Built solo using Kotlin + Jetpack Compose with MVVM architecture. Used Firebase Auth for role-based login (student vs admin), Firestore for real-time content delivery, and Coil for image loading. Implemented a Modal Navigation Drawer inspired by the official MAIT website UI.

Chess — Online Multiplayer Game

Personal Project
What

A real-time online multiplayer chess application with in-game chat, wallet system, match history, and player stats — built as a platform where users could potentially compete with stakes.

Why

Inspired by the idea of building a skill-based real-money gaming platform for chess — a game of pure logic and strategy. Wanted to create something that combined competitive gaming with financial incentives, akin to chess betting platforms globally.

Built with Kotlin using MVVM architecture and Android best practices. Integrated Firebase Realtime Database for live move sync enabling online multiplayer.
